ROLL lesson-
Demonstrate the C to C roll (right handed roll even left handed people- RT hand control paddle dictate so)
ROLL explained:
step 1 - Set up
	step 2 - Left thumb up the ass, right hand pushing down to the left
	step 3 - pull + hip snap + head down with rt elbow toward rt knee up + left elbow down

Be outside in the water help students in body positioning by putting them where they should be. Help with students FEELING WHERE their body position is.  DIFFICULT to cognitively explain & comprehend as everything is backwards and upside down.  Best to experience and feel where their body position should be. HOLD the paddle so students can come up x3-5.

If successful - try on own.  
If not successful - have them in step 2 while instructor hook student under right arm pit from behind.  Have the students try to roll up from this position.

NORMALIZE - new novel behavior. That way we don´t need to cognitively remember especially when trying the roll. 
When hip snaping, YOU HAVE to practice keeping head down so you can normalize the behavior so you can focus on other steps when doing the roll.
