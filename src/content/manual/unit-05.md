<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-8">
<span class="bg-[#4796cc] text-white px-3 py-1 rounded text-sm font-bold uppercase tracking-wider">Unit 5</span>
<h2 class="text-3xl font-bold serif m-0">
<span class="zh">課程組織與帶領</span>
<span class="en">Organizing and Conducting a Class</span>
</h2>
</div>

<p>
<span class="zh">課程的成功取決於細緻的準備。除了教學大綱，教練還必須考慮到人員移動（上下船）所需的時間，這通常比預期的要長。</span>
<span class="en">The success of a course depends on meticulous preparation. In addition to the lesson plan, instructors must account for class movement (getting in and out of the water), which often takes significantly longer than expected.</span>
</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-800 mt-10 mb-4 font-black">
<span class="zh">1. 教練的職責與素養</span>
<span class="en">1. Instructor Responsibilities & Qualities</span>
</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-600 mb-6">
<span class="zh">作為 ACA 教練，您的角色不僅是傳授技巧，更是維護組織形象與學員安全的關鍵。</span>
<span class="en">As an ACA Instructor, your role is more than teaching skills; you are key to maintaining the organization's image and student safety.</span>
</p>

<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6 mb-12">
<div class="bg-gray-50 p-6 rounded-2xl border border-gray-100">
<h4 class="font-bold text-gray-800 mb-3 text-sm"><span class="zh">主要職責：</span><span class="en">Key Duties:</span></h4>
<ul class="text-sm space-y-2 text-gray-600 list-none pl-0">
<li class="flex items-start gap-2"><span class="text-blue-500">•</span> <span><span class="zh">以積極形象代表 ACA</span><span class="en">Represent ACA positively</span></span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2"><span class="text-blue-500">•</span> <span><span class="zh">確保教學區域安全且無危險</span><span class="en">Ensure safe, hazard-free teaching areas</span></span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2"><span class="text-blue-500">•</span> <span><span class="zh">根據學員背景調整教學風格</span><span class="en">Adapt teaching styles to student backgrounds</span></span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2"><span class="text-blue-500">•</span> <span><span class="zh">完整覆蓋課程大綱內容</span><span class="en">Cover full course outline content</span></span></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="bg-gray-50 p-6 rounded-2xl border border-gray-100">
<h4 class="font-bold text-gray-800 mb-3 text-sm"><span class="zh">行政義務：</span><span class="en">Administrative Duties:</span></h4>
<ul class="text-sm space-y-2 text-gray-600 list-none pl-0">
<li class="flex items-start gap-2"><span class="text-blue-500">•</span> <span><span class="zh">向國家辦公室提交課程記錄</span><span class="en">Submit course records to National Office</span></span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2"><span class="text-blue-500">•</span> <span><span class="zh">發放學員卡或認證文件</span><span class="en">Issue student cards or certificates</span></span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2"><span class="text-blue-500">•</span> <span><span class="zh">指導並監督助教與志工</span><span class="en">Guide and supervise assistants/volunteers</span></span></li>
<li class="flex items-start gap-2"><span class="text-blue-500">•</span> <span><span class="zh">維持年度會員與 SEIC 註冊</span><span class="en">Maintain annual membership and SEIC registration</span></span></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-800 mt-10 mb-4 font-black">
<span class="zh">2. 裝備與課前準備</span>
<span class="en">2. Equipment and Preparation</span>
</h3>
<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-8 mb-12">
<div>
<h4 class="font-bold text-[#4796cc] mb-4 text-sm"><span class="zh">教練必備清單：</span><span class="en">Instructor Kit:</span></h4>
<ul class="text-sm space-y-2 list-disc list-inside pl-0 text-slate-600">
<li><span class="zh">適合該領域的船隻、槳與 PFD</span><span class="en">Discipline-appropriate boat, paddle, and PFD</span></li>
<li><span class="zh">急救箱、拋繩袋與救援系統</span><span class="en">First aid kit, throw bag, and recovery systems</span></li>
<li><span class="zh">通訊設備與法定安全器材</span><span class="en">Communication and legally required equipment</span></li>
<li><span class="zh">緊急修理工具箱</span><span class="en">Emergency repair kit</span></li>
<li><span class="zh">備用的保暖/乾燥衣物與飲水</span><span class="en">Spare warm/dry clothing and water</span></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="bg-amber-50 p-6 rounded-2xl border border-amber-100">
<h4 class="font-bold text-amber-800 mb-4 text-sm"><span class="zh">學員行前要求：</span><span class="en">Student Pre-course Requirements:</span></h4>
<ul class="text-sm space-y-2 text-amber-900 list-disc list-inside pl-0">
<li><span class="zh">適合水域活動的個人穿著</span><span class="en">Proper personal water attire</span></li>
<li><span class="zh">防曬、防蟲液與充足能量補充</span><span class="en">Sunscreen, bug spray, and hydration/snacks</span></li>
<li><span class="zh">簽署免責聲明與告知健康狀況</span><span class="en">Signed waiver and health status disclosure</span></li>
<li><span class="zh">遵守教練的安全指令</span><span class="en">Compliance with instructor safety directions</span></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-800 mt-12 mb-6">
<span class="zh">3. 場地選擇與比率</span>
<span class="en">3. Venue Selection and Ratios</span>
</h3>
<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6 mb-12">
<div class="space-y-3">
<div class="p-4 bg-slate-50 rounded-xl border border-slate-100">
<strong class="text-slate-900 text-sm block mb-1"><span class="zh">避風避浪：</span><span class="en">Sheltered Water:</span></strong> 
<span class="text-sm text-slate-500"><span class="zh">減少學習時的挫折感，選擇不超過學員能力的區域。</span><span class="en">Minimize frustration; choose areas within student capabilities.</span></span>
</div>
<div class="p-4 bg-slate-50 rounded-xl border border-slate-100">
<strong class="text-slate-900 text-sm block mb-1"><span class="zh">環境尊重：</span><span class="en">Environmental Respect:</span></strong> 
<span class="text-sm text-slate-500"><span class="zh">尊重私人土地與垂釣者，實踐 LNT 原則。</span><span class="en">Respect private land and anglers; practice LNT.</span></span>
</div>
</div>
<div class="bg-blue-900 text-white p-6 rounded-2xl flex flex-col justify-center text-center">
<h4 class="font-bold mb-2 text-blue-300 text-sm"><span class="zh">教練：學員比率</span><span class="en">Instructor-to-Student Ratios</span></h4>
<p class="text-xs opacity-80 leading-relaxed">
<span class="zh">推薦的比率可在官網查詢。適時利用「有能力的助手 (Able Assistant)」來維持比率要求。若學員有身障需求，建議增加輔助人力。</span>
<span class="en">Check official ratios online. Use "Able Assistants" to maintain safety standards. Additional assistance is recommended for participants with disabilities.</span>
</p>
</div>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-800 mt-12 mb-6">
<span class="zh">4. 教學實務與 M+D3 流程</span>
<span class="en">4. Teaching Best Practices & M+D3</span>
</h3>
<div class="space-y-6">
<div class="p-6 bg-blue-50/30 border border-blue-100 rounded-2xl">
<h4 class="font-bold text-blue-900 mb-4 flex items-center gap-2">
<svg class="w-5 h-5" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M14.752 11.168l-3.197-2.132A1 1 0 0010 9.87v4.263a1 1 0 001.555.832l3.197-2.132a1 1 0 000-1.664z"></path><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M21 12a9 9 0 11-18 0 9 9 0 0118 0z"></path></svg>
<span class="zh">M+D3 教學流程範式</span>
<span class="en">M+D3 Teaching Paradigm</span>
</h4>
<div class="grid grid-cols-2 md:grid-cols-4 gap-4 text-center">
<div class="bg-white p-3 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-blue-50">
<span class="block text-blue-600 font-black text-lg leading-none mb-1">M</span>
<span class="text-xs font-bold text-gray-700 block"><span class="zh">動機</span><span class="en">Motivation</span></span>
</div>
<div class="bg-white p-3 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-blue-50">
<span class="block text-blue-600 font-black text-lg leading-none mb-1">D1</span>
<span class="text-xs font-bold text-gray-700 block"><span class="zh">示範</span><span class="en">Demonstrate</span></span>
</div>
<div class="bg-white p-3 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-blue-50">
<span class="block text-blue-600 font-black text-lg leading-none mb-1">D2</span>
<span class="text-xs font-bold text-gray-700 block"><span class="zh">描述</span><span class="en">Describe</span></span>
</div>
<div class="bg-white p-3 rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-blue-50">
<span class="block text-blue-600 font-black text-lg leading-none mb-1">D3</span>
<span class="text-xs font-bold text-gray-700 block"><span class="zh">實作</span><span class="en">Do It</span></span>
</div>
</div>
<p class="text-xs text-gray-500 mt-4 leading-relaxed text-center">
<span class="zh">教練引導興趣 ➜ 準確示範 ➜ 口頭解析關鍵點 ➜ 學員立即訓練。</span>
<span class="en">Motivation ➜ Demonstration ➜ Description ➜ Practical Drills.</span>
</p>
</div>
<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">
<div class="p-5 bg-amber-50/30 border border-amber-100 rounded-2xl">
<h4 class="font-bold text-amber-900 mb-2 text-sm">☀️ <span class="zh">背對太陽原則</span><span class="en">Back-to-Sun Principle</span></h4>
<p class="text-sm text-amber-800 m-0 leading-relaxed">
<span class="zh">安排學員背對太陽站立，確保他們能清晰觀察示範而不會因刺眼而分心。</span>
<span class="en">Position students with backs to the sun to ensure clear visibility of demonstrations.</span>
</p>
</div>
<div class="p-5 bg-amber-50/30 border border-amber-100 rounded-2xl">
<h4 class="font-bold text-amber-900 mb-2 text-sm">👁️ <span class="zh">眼神與互動</span><span class="en">Eyes & Engagement</span></h4>
<p class="text-sm text-amber-800 m-0 leading-relaxed">
<span class="zh">保持眼神接觸以確保參與度，並即時察覺學員的恐懼或困惑。</span>
<span class="en">Maintain eye contact to ensure focus and detect signs of fear or confusion.</span>
</p>
</div>
</div>
