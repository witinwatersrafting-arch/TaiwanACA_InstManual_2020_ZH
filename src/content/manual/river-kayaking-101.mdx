        <h5 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-1">船隻調整</h5>
        <p class="text-sm text-slate-600 m-0 leading-relaxed">確認一般尺寸合適度，調整腳踏板 (Bulkhead)、背帶 (Back Band) 及座位。</p>
      </div>
    </div>

        <span class="inline-block px-2 py-1 bg-blue-600 text-white text-[10px] font-black rounded mb-2 uppercase">陸上</span>

        <p class="text-sm text-slate-600 m-0 leading-relaxed">指導員示範翻轉志願者，進行立即、5s、10s、15s、30s 計時練習。</p>

        <span class="inline-block px-2 py-1 bg-slate-400 text-white text-[10px] font-black rounded mb-2 uppercase">進出</span>
        <h5 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-1">水上進出練習</h5>
        <p class="text-sm text-slate-600 m-0 leading-relaxed">示範並練習如何進入與離開獨木舟。</p>
      </div>
      <div class="p-4 bg-slate-50 rounded-xl border border-slate-200/50">
        <span class="inline-block px-2 py-1 bg-slate-900 text-white text-[10px] font-black rounded mb-2 uppercase">排水</span>

        <p class="text-sm text-slate-600 m-0 leading-relaxed">練習如何排除船內積水。</p>

  </TimelineStep>

  <TimelineStep title="髖部翻正 (Hip Snaps)" time="45 MINS">

        <span class="inline-block px-2 py-1 bg-blue-600 text-white text-[10px] font-black rounded mb-2 uppercase">動作</span>

        <p class="text-sm text-slate-600 m-0 leading-relaxed">前後掃槳 (Sweeps) 練習，強調軀幹旋轉 (Rotation) 帶動划槳。</p>
