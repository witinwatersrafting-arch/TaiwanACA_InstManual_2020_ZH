            <p class="text-base text-amber-800 leading-relaxed m-0">
                <span class="zh">課程大綱通常只計算純教學時間。教練在規劃時必須額外算入：</span>
                <span class="en">Standard syllabi usually only account for direct instructional time. Instructors must also factor in:</span>
                <span class="block mt-2 font-bold">• <span class="zh">學員與裝備的移動</span><span class="en">Class movement and gear hauling</span></span>
                <span class="block">• <span class="zh">下水與上岸過程</span><span class="en">Launching and landing procedures</span></span>
                <span class="block">• <span class="zh">必要的休息、午餐與水分補充</span><span class="en">Necessary breaks, lunch, and hydration</span></span>
            </p>
        </div>
        <div class="w-full md:w-px bg-amber-200"></div>
        <div class="flex-1">
            <h4 class="text-amber-900 font-bold text-sm mb-2"><span class="zh">專業提醒</span><span class="en">Pro-Tip</span></h4>
            <p class="text-base text-amber-800 italic m-0">
                <span class="zh">「將學員從一個地點引導至另一個地點所需的時間，往往會顯著增加課程總時長。忽視這些隱形成本會導致教案延誤或跳過關鍵安全環節。」</span>
                <span class="en">"The time required to guide students from one point to another often adds significantly to the course duration. Ignoring these costs can lead to delays or the skipping of critical safety segments."</span>
            </p>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>

            <p class="text-base text-slate-500 m-0 leading-relaxed">
                <span class="zh">優點在於無危險、低干擾。讓學員能專注於精煉動作細節與執行策略，而不被強大的水流或風浪分散注意力。適合介紹新技能。</span>
                <span class="en">Minimal hazards and distractions. Allows students to focus on refining technical details and strategies without the stress of current or wind. Ideal for introducing new skills.</span>
            </p>
        </div>
        <div class="p-6 bg-slate-50 rounded-2xl border border-slate-100">
            <h4 class="font-bold text-slate-900 text-sm mb-2"><span class="zh">動態水域</span><span class="en">Dynamic Water Challenges</span></h4>
            <p class="text-base text-slate-500 m-0 leading-relaxed">
                <span class="zh">提供執行「決斷」與掌握「時機」的機會。當學員具備基礎技能後，應逐漸增加水域難度，以建立在真實環境中的應變能力。</span>
                <span class="en">Provides opportunities for decision-making and timing. Once basic skills are mastered, the environment should progressively increase in difficulty to build real-world adaptability.</span>
            </p>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>

            <h5 class="text-blue-400 font-bold text-sm"><span class="zh">課程簡介</span><span class="en">Course Intro</span></h5>
            <p class="text-sm opacity-70">
                <span class="zh">向學員解釋課程目標、安排與組織方式，讓學員知道「接下來會發生什麼」。</span>
                <span class="en">Explain goals, schedules, and organization so students know "what's coming next."</span>
            </p>
        </div>
        <div class="space-y-2">
            <h5 class="text-blue-400 font-bold text-sm"><span class="zh">責任與器材</span><span class="en">Ego & Equipment</span></h5>
            <p class="text-sm opacity-70">
                <span class="zh">確認學員了解如何挑選適合自己的器材，並強調維護裝備良好狀態的責任。</span>
                <span class="en">Ensure students know how to select proper gear and emphasize their responsibility for maintaining its condition.</span>
            </p>
        </div>
        <div class="space-y-2">
            <h5 class="text-blue-400 font-bold text-sm"><span class="zh">安全簡報</span><span class="en">Safety Briefing</span></h5>
            <p class="text-sm opacity-70">
                <span class="zh">概述潛在危險，協助學員進行個人風險評估。強調教練無法保證 100% 安全，需共同配合。</span>
                <span class="en">Outline hazards and assist in personal risk assessment. Clarify that 100% safety cannot be guaranteed; cooperation is key.</span>
            </p>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
